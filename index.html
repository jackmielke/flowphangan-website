Flow Phangan

Join our pop-up community of engineers and digital nomads for a month on the beautiful island of Koh Phangan

What

We want to gather builders and cool humans on the Thai island of Ko Phangan for 4 weeks, from April 18th to May 18th 2025. We already have a villa to use as informal coworking and gathering space but the rest is up to you.

A handful of people will stay in the villa itself, we encourage others to find their own lodging nearby. We are keeping things simple: a group chat for coordination, some community dinners and informal unconference-style sessions on weekdays. High-agency individuals welcome; we're not hand-holding.

Why Ko Phangan?

In our opinion, it's one of the best places in the world to nomad:

  • Low cost of living, excellent views and beaches, and easy to get modern amenities
  • Cost per meal of $2 - $6
  • The island culture is fantastic: Muy Thai, multiple ecstatic dances, an active yoga & conscious community
  • Accommodation on island for all budgets: from $400/mo bungalows to $10k/mo luxury villas (split with your friends!)

Who We Are

We're two remote-working devs who want to gather like-minded builders:

  • Atin: An experienced founder and engineer, working on a new startup
  • Jack: A developer focused on AI-augmented building, working as a remote engineering consultant

We're not running a conference; we're simply providing a temporal and physical anchor to gather good souls.
